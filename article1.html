5 Medical Innovations You Probably Didn’t Notice Happened in 2020

Overall, 2020 has been a tumultuous year. From a health perspective, it’s been one turned upside down with a deadly global pandemic reorienting how we live our lives and relate to others. The COVID-19 pandemic has justifiably dominated headlines and attention from media, policymakers, and health officials alike. While it’s clearly the defining public health, cultural, economic — even political — event of the year, the pandemic shouldn’t obscure the fact that 2020 was also a time of great medical innovation. From breakthroughs in oncology, gene therapies, and heart health, to the development of COVID-19 vaccines that are now being administered domestically and around the world, there’s a lot that the medical community can be proud of in 2020. Healthline touched base with leading experts about some of the most impactful medical advances of the year and how they hint at a more hopeful tomorrow.
